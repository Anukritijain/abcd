Dear Warriors, we understand how difficult it was for you and your family to fight COVID-19.

We are thrilled that you recovered from the widespread pandemic. Now that you are healthy, we request you to help who are still battling this disease. There is a dire need to plasma donors who can help fresh active patients to treat them before they go into a critical stage. Your donation can be used to treat hundreds of patients.

There is enough uncertainity in the world these days. We just want to bring back some order and ease the process of finding a match between a plasma donor and a patient in need.

Together, with collaboration and systematic support, we can eradicate this disease from our Nation.

STEP 1 - Registrations

As a Donor and a Patient, identify yourself and your requirements by filling up a form

STEP 2 - Smart Match Making

A patient is then matched with a prospective donor in the vicinity and the contact information is confidentially shared

STEP 3 - Donor Request

The patient can directly contact the matched donor to receive the plasma donation for their treatment
